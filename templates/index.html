Margin value: We send margin (price − random cost%) as the event value, plus price, currency, and pltv in custom_data.

Cost% Min/Max: Range used to generate a random item cost percentage. Tighter range = more stable margins.

Delay: Artificial server delay (0–3000ms) applied before sending to simulate latency.

Match-Rate Degrade: Higher numbers randomly remove hashed IDs to simulate lower match quality.

Currency: “Auto” picks USD. “Null” literally sends null.
